# Project Brief: Syakira Printing Web Design

## 1. Project Overview
Syakira Printing is a professional printing company specializing in high-quality digital, offset, and large format printing. The project aims to create a modern, professional, and user-friendly web presence that showcases their diverse product catalog and facilitates online ordering for both retail and corporate clients.

## 2. Brand Identity
- **Brand Name:** Syakira Printing
- **Primary Color:** Teal / Biru Toska (#00a3ad)
- **Visual Style:** Professional, clean, minimalist, and precise ("Precision in every pixel").
- **Language:** Bahasa Indonesia (Primary).

## 3. Core Objectives
- Showcase a wide range of printing services (Digital, Large Format, Offset).
- Highlight retail products: Custom Tote Bags, Apparel (T-shirts), Premium Tumblers, and Wedding Invitations.
- Provide a clear and intuitive "Pesan Online" (Online Ordering) flow.
- Ensure easy access to contact information and physical location.

## 4. Site Map & Screen Requirements
1.  **Beranda (Home):** Hero section with value proposition, "Produk Unggulan" (Featured Products) showcase using high-quality product photography, and clear CTAs.
2.  **Katalog Layanan (Service Catalog):** A comprehensive grid view of all services and products with pricing and descriptions.
3.  **Pesan Online (Online Ordering):** A functional multi-step form for clients to input details, select service categories, upload assets (.PDF, .AI, .ZIP), and provide special instructions.
4.  **Hubungi Kami (Contact Us):** Landscape layout featuring direct contact details (Email, WhatsApp), operating hours, and an integrated interactive map.

## 5. Visual Assets
- **Product Photography:** Professional studio-style images with consistent lighting and teal brand accents.
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (Hanken Grotesk) for readability and a contemporary feel.
- **Components:** Sticky navigation bar, descriptive footer, and interactive cards for services.

## 6. Target Audience
- **Retail Customers:** Individuals looking for custom gifts (tumblers, bags) or wedding invitations.
- **Corporate Clients:** Businesses needing high-volume offset printing or marketing materials.
- **Local Market:** Primary focus on the Indonesian market with localized content.
